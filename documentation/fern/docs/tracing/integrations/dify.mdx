Learn how to connect GPTHOUSE with Dify to monitor your applications' performance.

## Setup Instructions

Follow these simple steps to connect Dify with GPTHOUSE:

1. Select the Dify app you want to monitor
2. Select **Monitoring** from the side menu
3. Click on **Tracing app performance**
4. Click on **Configure** for GPTHOUSE
5. Enter your connection details based on your GPTHOUSE version:

<Frame>
  <img src="/img/tracing/dify_configuration.png" />
</Frame>

<Tabs>
<Tab value="Hosted GPTHOUSE" title="Hosted GPTHOUSE">

Fill in these fields:

- **API Key**: Your Comet API Key
- **Project**: Your preferred project name (if left empty, it will be created automatically)
- **Workspace**: Your Comet Workspace name (must already exist)
- **URL**: Your GPTHOUSE installation URL (make sure it ends with `/api/`)

</Tab>

<Tab value="Self-Hosted GPTHOUSE" title="Self-Hosted GPTHOUSE">

Fill in these fields:

- **API Key**: Leave this empty
- **Project**: Your preferred project name (if left empty, it will be created automatically)
- **Workspace**: Type `default`
- **URL**: Your GPTHOUSE installation URL (make sure it ends with `/api/`)

</Tab>

</Tabs>

## How to View Your Traces

After setup, you can view your application traces by:

1. Opening the **Monitoring** section from the side menu
2. Finding and clicking the **GPTHOUSE** button in the top-right corner
3. Selecting **View** to open your GPTHOUSE project dashboard
